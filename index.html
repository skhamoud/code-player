CodePlayer

CodePlayer is a simple beautifully designed very responsive HTML and CSS playground

What is so cool about CodePlayer?

Wether you are on a desktop with a huge screen, or a Tablet 800 pixels wide or even a handheld smartphone, CodePlayer will adjust it's layout to your need.

CodePlayer works great whatever device you're on. It was coded with performance and efficiency in mind.

Modern design and feel is obvious in CodePlayer, it represents simplicity and usability.
